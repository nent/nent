View Lists

Where are you in the scheme of things?

How it Works

The <n-view-link-list > will create a list with anchors showing where you are and what is around you depending on the "mode" attribute selected (parents, children, siblings). There are more specific attributes available on the <n-view-link-list > page. This is especially useful for navbars, menus, sub menus, breadcrumbs and other wayfinding needs.
