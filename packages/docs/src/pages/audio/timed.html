Timed Sounds

This page has a timed sound configured to play after being on the page for 5 seconds.

Play a sound at a certain time!

First: Preload

To get the timing perfect, again we pre-load the sound using the <n-audio-action-sound-load> component.

Then: Activate by Time

We play the sound by sending an action with start command after 5 seconds. We use the <n-action-activator> element and <n-audio-action-sound> elements with the command and track-id.
